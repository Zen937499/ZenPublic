Refund Policy

ZenGrid Cloud Services Not Provide Refund. For monthly or any other term of billing, we offer 7 days Money Back Guarantee. Once you cancel the service refund will be made within 10 days and if accounts canceled/terminated by ZenGrid Cloud Services due to any violation of Terms or AUP do not qualify for the 7 or 15 days money back guarantee. Refunds will be not provided if the service is used to test your program/script and cancel when it is done. Refunds are only provided if the server environment is incompatible or unable to provide the features required for your program/script or any issue which ZenGrid Cloud Services staff cannot solve. The following methods of payment are non-refundable, and refunds will be posted as credit to the hosting account for current or future Services: bank wire transfers, Western Union payments, checks, and money orders.

Like all payments, all refunds are processed in U.S. dollars, and will reflect the exchange rate in effect on the date of the refund. All refunds are subject to this fluctuation and ZenGrid Cloud Services is not responsible for any change in exchange rates between time of payment and time of refund. In addition, ZenGrid Cloud Services reserves the right to refuse a refund at any time for any or no reason.
